About me
Passionate visual artist specializing in illustration and comics, and a seasoned social communicator with expertise in editorial publishing. I excel at crafting visually engaging narratives and thrive on exploring the storytelling potential of drawing. Known for working swiftly, I am an adaptable problem-solver, eager to learn and contribute to creative projects.
